YGO Melffy Combos





*Only some lists run this card

Relevant Combos

Obedience Schooled Combo

(Potential Endboards)

1. Use Obedience Schooled.
2. SS Wally, Kittytail, and any other third you prefer.
3. Package all three into Joyous Melffy.
**Toggle effect activations to "ON"**
*End Turn*
4. In the draw phase, Joyous effect to SS Kittytail + Wally.
5. Kittytail effect targets the third beast you left in the GY. Send Peropero Cerperus to GY.
- Whenever you take damage from battle/effect, you can banish Cerperus from the GY and pop one card on the field.
- If/when Kittytail is destroyed by an opponent's card, you can search any follow up in your deck.
When triggered:
1. Wally SS Catty + Puppy.
2. Catty search Pinny.
3. Puppy SS Kalantosa, pop one card on the field.
4. Pinny effect SS self to board.
5. Pinny SS Accel Synchro using all Melffy in hand.
6. Accel Synchro effect > SS Pinny > Stardust Dragon.
7. Stardust Dragon effect into Satellite Warrior.
8. Satellite effect to pop 1~2 cards on the field, gaining attack for each one that is actually destroyed.
- Does not gain attack if the destruction does not resolve on the target.
- I.E. S:P or other effects

Merry Melffys > Forest Negate + Kalantosa

(Bounce one monster to hand, SS Forest for /2000 body + 1x permanent attack/effect negate. Kalantosa SS pop any card on the field.)

1. NS Wally to bait handtraps and avoid SS in end phase.
2. **End Turn**
*When triggered:*
3. Wally SS Catty + Puppy
4. Catty search Pinny
5. Pinny effect SS self to board.
6. Pinny > Catty into Merry Melffys.
7. Merry Melffys bounce one monster to hand on SS.
8. Puppy effect to SS Kalantosa.
- Chain 1
9. Merry Melffys effect to SS Melffy of the Forest.
- Chain 2
10. Forest lands.
11. Puppy to hand. SS Kalantosa.
12. Kalantosa effect triggers. Pop one card on the field.
13. Forest effect activates. Choose one target to permanently negate the attack/effects of.
- Forest effect can only ever be C1. Due to this, to get max value you will need to pop 1 card and still have an enemy to negate. Typically, this means using Kalantosa on backrow or, ideally, when engine two bodies are on board.
