Relevant Combos

4 Interruption

1. SS Witch from hand. Activate effect to search Spoils.
2. Spoils > Witch > SS Ash
3. Ash > Ponix. Ponix NS, search Sanc.
4. Sanc activate, search Island.
5. Island activate, search Garunix.
6. Garunix Activate. SS, pop Kirin from deck/hand.
7. SS Ponix from GY.
8. Ash effect to send self + Ponix, SS Flamberg.
9. Flamberg + Garunix into Wolf.
10. Flamberg effect to SS Ponix + Ash (to the zone Wolf points to)
11. Wolf effect to search Kirin.
12. Ash into Linkuriboh. 3 bodies (Not Linkuriboh) into Princess.
13. Princess effect SS Flamberg. Flamberg witch to trap zone.
14. Linkuriboh + Princess into Amblowhale.
End Turn
Opponent's Turn:
1. Flamberg SS Witch from trap zone. Chain 2 Kirin.
2. Kirin SS self, sending Flamberg. Witch is SS.
3. Garunix C1, Flamberg C2, Witch C3.
- Witch sets a spell you prefer.
- Flamberg SS Ponix + Ash
- Garunix SS self.
4. Garunix C1, Ponix C2, Ash C3
- Ash searches (Oak, Kurikara, Poplar... depends on list)
- Ponix searches Circle
- Garunix sends Arvata to GY.
5. Garunix + Kirin into Garunix Eternity XYZ. XYZ effect to destroy all monsters.
6. Chain cover Whale effect with Witch.
- Witch sends set spell you searched earlier with her effect to SS self.
- Whale SS Wolf
7. Princess pops Garunix XYZ + Opponent Monster
8. Garunix from GY to SS Arvata + Kirin from GY.
9. Kirin effect from GY to SS Ponix.

Amblowhale

1. NS Poplar to search Spoils.
2. Link Poplar into Linkuriboh and set self in trap zone.
3. Spoils > Poplar > SS Ponix.
4. Ponix effect to search Sanc. Sanc sets Island.
5. Island effect to destroy Ponix, search Garunix, and SS self.
6. Garunix to destroy Arvata. GY effect to SS Ponix.
7. Garunix + Ponix into Hiita.
8. Hiita + Linkuriboh into Princess. SS Poplar.
9. Poplar + Princess into whale.
End Turn
Opponent's Turn:
1. Princess pops whale + 1 enemy monster, SS self.
2. SS Hiita with Whale, SS Garunix with Garunix.
3. Garunix sends Kirin to GY. Kirin SS Arvata and destroys one card on field.
4. Arvata negates monster effect, sends Princess to GY.
5. Banish Whale from GY to pop Arvata.
6. Arvata from GY to SS Kirin. Sanc effect to XYZ Garunix + Kirin into Garunix XYZ. Destroys all monsters.
