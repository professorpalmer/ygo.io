8. Oak effect to summon back Poplar.

14. Hita + Flamberge > Selene.
15. Remove 3 counters to SS Diab from the GY.
16. Link Selene + Diab into Apollo.
End Turn.
Opponents Turn:
1. Activate Divine Temple to SS Flamberge from the spell/trap zone.
2. Activate to SS I:P Masq.
