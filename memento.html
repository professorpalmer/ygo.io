Memento Deck Combos!





Relevant Combos

Dark Blade/Angwitch to 10 Disruptions

1. Normal Summon Dark Blade and activate it to destroy itself and summon Angwitch from the deck.
2. Angwitch searches for Horse. Special Summon Horse and activate it to destroy itself and send Mace, Goblin, and Ghattic from the deck to the grave.
3. Ghattic triggers when sent to the grave to special summon itself. Do not activate it yet.
4. Activate Angwitch to destroy itself to summon Mace from the grave.
5. Activate Mace to destroy itself to search for Memento Fusion.
6. Activate Memento Fusion.
7. Fuse into Twin Dragon using Horse and Dark Blade.
8. Activate Twin Dragon to destroy itself to search for Combined Creation and Horned Dragon.

Unprotected 6 Disruptions

1. Normal Summon Goblin.
2. Activate it to destroy itself to send Ghattic and Memento Fusion from deck to grave.
3. Activate Ghattic to special summon itself.
4. Activate Ghattic on summon to grab Memento Fusion.
5. Activate it to fuse into Twin Dragon using Ghattic on field and Goblin in grave.
6. Twin Dragon activates to destroy itself to search for combined creation and horse.
7. Twin Dragon in grave will activate to summon Ghattic from grave, special summon horse.
8. Activate it to destroy itself to send Goblin, Mace, and Angwitch from deck to grave.
9. Activate Memento Fusion in grave to destroy Ghattic to search for Cranium Burst.
10. Set it.

CC + Field Spell + Trap

1. Special summon horse.
2. Activate it to destroy itself to send Mace, Goblin, and Ghattic from deck to grave.
3. Activate Ghattic to summon itself.
4. Activate Ghattic on summon to summon Mace from grave.
5. Normal Summon Mace.
6. Activate Mace to destroy itself to search for Memento Fusion.
7. Activate Memento Fusion.
8. Fuse into Twin Dragon using Horus and Mace.
9. On Summon, Twin Dragon will destroy Ghattic to search for Horned Dragon and Combined Creation.
10. Activate Memento Fusion in Grave to destroy Twin Dragon to search for the Field Spell.
11. Twin Dragon when destroyed will special summon Goblin.
12. Activate Field Spell.
13. Activate Goblin to destroy itself to send Cranium Burst and Elwich from Deck to Grave.
14. Summon Combined Creation by shuffling three monsters from grave to deck except one, as well as Horse and Horned Dragon in hand.
15. End your turn.
16. During the end phase, Field spell sets Cranium Burst, and you have 5 monster negates and Mace to steal a monster.

Protected 6 Disruptions

1. NS Goblin, effect to destroy self.
2. Goblin effect to send Ghattic and Bone Party to the GY.
3. SS Ghattic, use effect to target Bone Party.
4. Bone Party to pop Ghattic and SS Dark Blade.
5. Dark Blade effect to pop self and SS Angwitch.
6. Angwitch effect to fish Horse.
7. Horse effect to SS self. Horse effect to pop self.
8. Throw Mace, Goblin, Angwitch into the GY.
9. Angwitch on board effect to pop self and SS Mace.
10. Mace effect to pop self and fish Fusion.
11. Fusion into Twin Dragon using Horse and Angwitch.
12. Twin Dragon effect to target self, adding Horned Dragon and Combined Creation.
13. Twin Dragon GY effect to SS Ghattic from the GY.
14. SS Horn Dragon and link into Spright Elf.
15. Spright Elf effect to SS Ghattic from the GY.
16. Fusion effect in GY to banish + pop Ghattic. Fish Cranium Burst.
17. SS Combined Creation in defense mode by targeting:
- Twin Dragon
- Horned Dragon
- Goblin
- Mace
- Dark Blade
18. Set Cranium Burst.

Unprotected 6 + Field Spell

1. Normal Summon Goblin: Activate his effect to destroy itself, pop itself to send Ghattic and Fusion.
2. Special Summon Ghattic: Activate his effect to target fusion in the GY, add it to your hand.
3. Activate Fusion using Ghattic on field and Goblin in GY to make Twin Dragon.
4. Twin Dragon pop itself to search Tatsu and Combined Creation from the deck to the hand.
5. Twin Dragon in the GY special summon Ghattic of the GY.
6. Activate 2nd effect of Fusion in GY to pop Ghattic and add the field spell. Activate field spell.
7. Special summon horse and activate his effect to destroy itself, send Mace and Dark Blade/Witch (both works but D Blade can float into Witch to provide an extra body to Creation)
8. Activate field spell to target horse and summon Dark Blade (assuming that you send Dark Blade)
9. Dark Blade enter the field. Activate Dark Blade effect to pop itself and SS Angwitch.
10. Activate Angwitch's effect on summon to search a memento monster, add Horned Dragon.
11. Activate Angwitch's effect to tag out with Mace in the GY. Activate Mace's effect to pop itself, search Fracture Dance or Cranium Burst (the one that suits you better)
12. SS Creation, set the trap you search and pass the turn.
That way you have possibly more bodies on GY to summon with Creation + field + the trap you added to the hand + follow-up.
