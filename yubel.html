Yubel Combos





Relevant Combos

Combo 1: 8 Disruptions

1. Activate Throne, search Lotus.
2. SS Lotus, NS Spirit.
3. SET Nightmare Pain (Plays around Droll)
4. Activate Nightmare Pain, destroy Spirit to search Squirmer.
5. Spirit CL1, Throne CL2.
- Add Terror to hand, SS it.
- Spirit SS
6. Link into Cross Sheep.
7. SS Phantom of Yubel returning cards to the deck from the GY to a spot the Cross Sheep points to.
- SS Lotus from Sheep effect.
8. SS Squirmer and use Squirmer + Lotus to link into Yama.
9. Link all 3 into Apollo.
10. Squirmer effect to SS Spirit. Pop Spirit with Sharvara to SS.
11. Spirit effect in GY to SS Yubel from deck. Link into Unchained Rage.
12. Sharvara effect to set Escape of the Unchained.
13. SS Phantom by returning Yubel to the deck from the GY.

Combo 2: 8 Disruptions (Weak vs Nibiru)

2. Set N Pain, pop Spirit to search Squirmer.
3. Spirit SS Yubel. Squirmer SS self and pop Yubel.

5. SS Phantom to cross sheep arrow. Reborn Lotus.
6. SS Yama. Link into 1600 Apollo.
7. Squirmer effect SS Spirit from GY.
8. Sharvara effect pops Spirit to SS Yubel.
- Link into Rage.
- Sharvara sets Escape.
9. SS Phantom.

Combo 3: 4 Disruptions

1. NS Beast, search Spirit Gates.
2. Spirit Gates search another Beast.
3. NS Beast a second time. Link into Yama.
4. Add Sharvara to hand.
5. Spirit Gates effect to discard Sharvara and SS Beast.
6. Sharvara sets Escape.
7. Link into Rage.

13. Squirmer SS and pop Yubel.
14. SS Terror, link into second Yama.
15. SS Phantom from GY bodies.

17. Unchained Shyama pop Spirit to SS itself.

19. Link Yubel, Phantom, Yama into Gryphon.
20. Discard 1 card. Set Escape. Draw 1.
21. SS Phantom via GY effect. SS Phantom via GY effect.
22. Link Unchained Soul + Phantom into rage.

Combo 5: 7 Disruptions

1. NS Lotus, tribute to SS Spirit of Yubel from deck.
2. Spirit sets Nightmare Pain from deck (if not in hand already).
3. Activate Nightmare Pain, destroy Spirit to search Gruesome Grave Squirmer.
4. Spirit effect SS Yubel from deck.
5. SS Squirmer, use effect to destroy Yubel.
6. Yubel effect SS Terror Incarnate from deck.
7. Link Squirmer and Terror Incarnate into Cross Sheep.
8. SS Phantom of Yubel by shuffling Yubel and Terror Incarnate back to deck, summon to Cross Sheep's link arrow.
9. Cross Sheep effect SS Lotus from grave.
10. Activate Squirmer effect to SS Spirit of Yubel from grave.
11. Link Phantom of Yubel and Lotus into Yama.
12. Yama effect to search Sharvara.
13. Link Yama and Cross Sheep into Apollousa.
14. Activate Sharvara to destroy Spirit of Yubel and SS itself.
15. CL1: Spirit, CL2: Yama
- Yama SS Spirit of Yubel from grave, then destroys it.
- Spirit SS Yubel from deck.
16. The destroyed Spirit SS another Yubel from deck.
17. Link Yubel and Sharvara into Unchained Soul of Rage.
18. Sharvara effect to set Escape of the Unchained from deck.
19. SS Phantom of Yubel by shuffling Yubel and Spirit of Yubel from grave back to deck.
20. End phase: Lotus SS itself from grave.
21. Yubel destroys itself.
